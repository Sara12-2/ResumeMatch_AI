Job Title: DevOps Engineer

About the Role
We need a DevOps Engineer to own our CI/CD pipelines and cloud
infrastructure as we scale our Kubernetes-based platform.

Responsibilities
- Design and maintain Terraform modules for AWS infrastructure.
- Operate and scale our Kubernetes clusters.
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions or Jenkins).
- Automate configuration management with Ansible.
- Write Bash scripts and maintain Linux servers.
- Set up monitoring and alerting for production systems.

Required Qualifications
- 3+ years of experience in a DevOps or SRE role.
- Strong experience with Docker and Kubernetes.
- Hands-on experience with Terraform and AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda).
- Experience with CI/CD tools such as GitHub Actions or Jenkins.
- Comfortable with Linux administration and Bash scripting.

Nice to Have
- Experience with Ansible or a similar configuration management tool.
- Familiarity with GCP or Azure in addition to AWS.

Education
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or equivalent experience.
