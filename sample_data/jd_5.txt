Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer

About the Role
We are hiring a Machine Learning Engineer to design, train, and deploy
models that power our recommendation system.

Responsibilities
- Build and train models using Python, scikit-learn, and PyTorch.
- Design data pipelines with Pandas and NumPy for feature engineering.
- Deploy models to production using Docker and Kubernetes.
- Monitor model performance and retrain as needed.
- Collaborate with data engineers on AWS-based infrastructure.

Required Qualifications
- 3+ years of experience building machine learning models in Python.
- Strong knowledge of scikit-learn, PyTorch, or TensorFlow.
- Experience with Pandas and NumPy for data processing.
- Familiarity with Docker and cloud deployment (AWS preferred).
- Solid understanding of statistics and model evaluation.

Nice to Have
- Experience with NLP libraries such as spaCy or NLTK.
- Familiarity with Kubernetes for model serving at scale.

Education
Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field
preferred.
