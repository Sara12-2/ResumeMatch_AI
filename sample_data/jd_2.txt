Job Title: Data Analyst

About the Role
We're hiring a Data Analyst to support our finance and marketing teams
with reporting, forecasting, and ad-hoc analysis.

Responsibilities
- Write SQL queries against our data warehouse to answer business
  questions.
- Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau or Power BI.
- Use Python (pandas) for data cleaning and forecasting models.
- Perform statistical analysis, including A/B test evaluation.
- Present insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders.

Required Qualifications
- 2+ years of experience in a data analyst or similar role.
- Strong SQL skills.
- Experience with Tableau or Power BI.
- Working knowledge of Python for data analysis (pandas, NumPy).
- Comfortable with Excel for quick analysis.
- Excellent communication skills.

Nice to Have
- Experience with forecasting or financial modeling.
- Familiarity with a cloud data warehouse (e.g. Snowflake, BigQuery).

Education
Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, or a related quantitative field.
